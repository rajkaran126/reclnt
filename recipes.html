Creamy Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Parmesan cheese, salt, pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta until al dente.
  2. Saute garlic, add cream, stir until thick.
  3. Mix pasta with sauce, sprinkle parmesan.

Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Add egg and milk, whisk until smooth.
  3. Cook on medium heat until golden.

Fresh Garden Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Combine greens, tomatoes, cucumber, and onion in a bowl.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, toss gently, and serve fresh.
